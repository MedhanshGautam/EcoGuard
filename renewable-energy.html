The Power of Renewables

Transitioning from fossil fuels to renewable energy is the most effective way to reduce greenhouse gas emissions. Renewables are now the cheapest form of new electricity in most of the world.

Solar Power

Harnessing energy directly from the sun using photovoltaic cells. In just one hour, the sun provides enough energy to power the entire world for a year.

Wind Energy

Wind turbines convert kinetic energy into electricity. Offshore wind farms have the potential to produce massive amounts of clean power with minimal land use.

Geothermal

Utilizing the Earth's internal heat to provide a constant, reliable source of "baseload" power that doesn't depend on weather conditions.

Why it Matters

  • ✅ **Zero Emissions**: Unlike coal or gas, renewables produce no CO2 during operation.
  • ✅ **Energy Security**: Diversified domestic energy sources reduce reliance on imported fuels.
  • ✅ **Job Creation**: The green energy sector creates 3x more jobs than the fossil fuel industry.
