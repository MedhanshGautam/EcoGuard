Preserving Biodiversity

Nature is our greatest ally. Healthy ecosystems—from Amazon rainforests to coastal mangroves—act as massive carbon sinks, absorbing up to 30% of human-caused CO2 emissions.

Ecosystem Services

Biodiversity provides us with clean water, pollination for our crops, and natural flood defenses. Losing a single species can disrupt an entire food chain.

The Extinction Crisis

Current extinction rates are 100 to 1,000 times higher than natural background levels. We are currently in the middle of the "Sixth Mass Extinction."

Restore & Rewild

Rewilding projects aim to restore natural processes and core wilderness areas, allowing ecosystems to self-regulate and recover from human impact.

Conservation Strategies

1. Protected Areas: Expanding national parks and marine reserves.
2. Sustainable Forestry: Ending clear-cutting and promoting regenerative harvests.
3. Urban Greening: Bringing nature back into our cities to support local pollinators.
